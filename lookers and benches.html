Lookers & benches


HPL Locker System is ultimate in locker design and construction. It does not rust and rot or disintegrate like steel, wood, and plastic lockers. Our 100% solid phenol board construction combines with the state-of-the-art hardware design gives you an extensive range of customized styles and sizes for your individual storage needs. Be it a changing room, library, school, swimming pool and gym. HPD HPL Lockers are waterproof, highly vandal resistant, elegant and versatile in performance.


HPL Lockers Specification

HPL Lockers are made of 100% solid phenol board using the best combination of design and the state-of-the-art technology resulting in an extensive range of styles and sizes for all your individual storage needs and custom design.


HPL Locker as supplies by HPD shall comprise the following :


  • Door shall be 13mm thick high pressure laminated (HPL).
  • Side, top, bottom and internal shelf shall be 13mm thick high pressure laminated (HPL).
  • Back shall be 6mm medium density fiberboard (MDF).
  • Lockers shall be construed with 4 aluminum interlocking supporting framework â€“ making it strong and rigid.
  • Concealed hinges shall be of Stainless Steel Grade 304, with steel numbering plate.
  • Standard dimensions of lockers unit shall be 300mm (width) x 400mm (depth) x 1800mm (height).

HPL BENCH

HPL Bench solution comprises a range of sturdy frame options which can be cleverly configured in different ways to maximize the space available. Having a variety of benches for changing rooms and public areas to complement the range of fixtures and fittings and manufactured with HPL waterproof in plank or slat formats. Each with smooth edges and available in a wide range of sporting colors.

Wall-mounted benches, benches with shoe rack, hangers for clothes and bags, single and double units. Benches in standard sizes and with the option of manufacturing in any size on request by the customer. Changing room benches made with 13mm thickness compact grade laminate.

Each standard frame is fabricated in strong mild steel section and painted in a light grey powder coated finish making them suitable for use in wet or dry changing rooms. 40mm x 40mm powder coated tubular steel-based structure, finished off with black feet and legs which are adjustable in height.
