WHO WE ARE :


Object interior (OID) is an ISO certified company with expertise management aiming to provide the latest, most, and high quality advanced products and methods for both interior and exterior works.



We are the leading manufacturer of High Pressure Laminated (HPL) products most especially Toilet Cubicle Partitions and Integrated Plumbing System (IPS). OID brings you to a garden of natural colors with branded Italian quality water based decorative art paints. We also provide the service of Islamic and Italian style of art designing, decoration and painting that stretches beyond the boundaries of interior designing.
